Flickr Fixr version

 Toggle features 

Up until 2012 Flickr had always updated the full database of photos when new browsing-sizes was introduced on the site. However in May 2012 only photos uploaded after March 2012 was updated when Flickr introduced 1600px and 2048px wide browsing-sizes, leaving the majority of photos to be displayed only 1024px wide. Flickr Fixr fixes this by scaling, and when the original is available, using the original uploaded photo for best possible quality.

Puts a column featuring albums on photographer's photostream. The 10 first albums as sorted in Flickr Organizer are shown in the column.

Adds an extra direct link in tags, pointing to the same photographer's similar tagged photos.

Adds two extra links in "album headers". One link pointing to a map with current album's photos, and another link pointing to the old hidden/forgotten page with album comments, which also is a great page for reading long album descriptions.

Adds the photo you came from to the image-ribbon, when opening Flickr Map on a photo. Also "opens" the photo, but it's a 90% fix: To see exactly where it was geotagged, click twice on the photo in the image-ribbon to close and re-open it; Now a star showing the exact position of photo appears. Bonus: Also adds a link to show geoposition on Google Maps.

Adds a pagination-bar when hovering mouse just in top of the photostreams, albumpages, favorite collections, ("beta experience") group pools and on the Explore page.

On the Explore page, add a link to the hidden/forgotten Explore Calendar and to page showing some of the latest uploads to Flickr. A little warning regarding the latest upload though. Even though Safe Search is activated on that page, you risk seeing stuff you might have preferred not to see.

Adds Tags, Collections and Map items to both the user ("You" dropdown) and the photographer menus (menu bar).

Via mouseover, show both Taken and Upload dates in detail when available. Taken date will also work as a link to more photos from the same photographer.

When browsing a photostream in Date takenem> order, a subtle visual hint will warn you that the latest added photos might not be on top of photostream.

If you have your Firefox configured to open tabs in the "background" when ctrl-clicking links, you may have seen Flickr overriding this preference on search result pages (including the Trending Tags pages) and bring immediately focus to new tabs instead. This should prevent Flickr overriding your open tabs preferences. I have only seen the problem when using Firefox, so feature is default disabled for other browsers.

Removes layer over images on "All sizes" pages, so you can use your browser's context menu for images. I have been silent about this feature, because I don't want Flickr Fixr to be a tool for people who want to use other peoples photos without permission. But I have image tools like "Search Similar image" and "EXIF viewer" which I would like to access via the context menu. Please respect ownership of images, and remember to ask permission for any public use of other peoples photos, if not already granted by an attached license.

You will have to reload any already open Flickr webpages to enable updated settings.
